## Decisions Made
- **hadTrigActivity flag:** Added `hadTrigActivity bool` to `buttonState` to distinguish ServerTravel zeroing (all buttons AND triggers went zero after having been active) from normal single-button releases (triggers never active). Without this, releasing the last button would falsely trigger hold.
- **Button constants in uinput.go:** Button codes (btnA, btnB, etc.) and axis codes (absZ, absRZ) are defined in `uinput.go` alongside kernel ABI types, since they're shared between uinput device creation (Plan 01) and dead reckoning tracking (Plan 02).
- **invertY in uinput.go:** Y-axis inversion is a low-level evdev utility shared across the proxy, so it lives in uinput.go rather than proxy.go.
